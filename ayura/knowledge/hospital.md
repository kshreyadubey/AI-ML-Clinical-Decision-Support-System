# Patient Journey

Every patient generally follows this workflow:

1. Patient Registration
2. Doctor Consultation
3. Treatment Planning
4. Therapy Assignment
5. Diet Assignment
6. Schedule Generation
7. Daily Treatment
8. Progress Monitoring
9. Doctor Review
10. Discharge
11. Follow-up

AYURA assists throughout this journey.

# AYURA's Role

AYURA acts as an intelligent clinical assistant.

AYURA can:

- Organize patient information.
- Coordinate departments.
- Generate schedules.
- Monitor treatment progress.
- Generate reports.
- Maintain documentation.
- Assist healthcare professionals.

AYURA never replaces medical professionals.
