# AYURA Glossary

## Purpose

This document defines the standard terminology used throughout AYURA.

The glossary ensures that all users, workflows, documents, and AI reasoning use consistent meanings for healthcare, operational, and system-related terms.

---

# Core Principle

Every important term should have one clear meaning.

AYURA should interpret terminology consistently throughout the system.

---

# General Terms

## AYURA

The AI Clinical Intelligence Platform that assists healthcare professionals by managing workflows, documentation, scheduling, monitoring, and clinical support.

---

## Patient

An individual receiving healthcare services from the institute.

---

## Healthcare Professional

An authorized user responsible for providing patient care.

Examples include:

- Doctor
- Yoga Therapist
- Dietitian
- Acupuncture Specialist

---

## Developer

An authorized technical user responsible for maintaining and improving the AYURA platform.

---

# Clinical Terms

## Consultation

A scheduled interaction between a doctor and a patient for assessment, review, or treatment planning.

---

## Treatment Plan

The complete plan of care prepared by the doctor for a patient.

It may include:

- Therapies
- Diet
- Follow-ups
- Recommendations

---

## Therapy

A structured healthcare intervention prescribed as part of a treatment plan.

---

## Follow-up

A future consultation or review to evaluate patient progress.

---

## Progress

The patient's improvement or response to treatment over time.

---

# Workflow Terms

## Appointment

A scheduled healthcare interaction between a patient and a healthcare professional.

---

## Schedule

An organized timeline of appointments, therapies, and consultations assigned to a patient or healthcare professional.

---

## Workflow

A sequence of activities performed to complete a healthcare process.

Example:

Registration

↓

Consultation

↓

Treatment

↓

Review

↓

Discharge

---

## Documentation

Structured information recorded during patient care.

Examples include:

- Consultation Notes
- Therapy Notes
- Diet Notes
- Progress Notes

---

## Report

A structured summary generated from patient, clinical, or operational data.

---

# AI Terms

## Recommendation

A non-binding suggestion generated by AYURA to assist healthcare professionals.

Recommendations support decision-making but never replace clinical judgment.

---

## Monitoring

Continuous observation of workflows, appointments, therapies, documentation, and patient progress.

---

## Notification

A message generated by AYURA to inform users about important events, reminders, updates, or alerts.

---

## Alert

A high-priority notification requiring immediate attention.

---

## Audit

A permanent record of significant actions performed within AYURA.

Audit records support accountability and traceability.

---

# Permission Terms

## View

Permission to read information.

---

## Create

Permission to add new information.

---

## Update

Permission to modify existing information.

---

## Approve

Permission to authorize important actions.

---

# System Terms

## Knowledge

The information AYURA uses during reasoning.

---

## Memory

Information AYURA remembers during and across workflows.

---

## Reasoning

The process AYURA uses to analyze information, make decisions, and generate recommendations.

---

## Workflow Engine

The component responsible for executing healthcare workflows.

---

## Clinical Intelligence

The combination of healthcare knowledge, workflow understanding, and AI reasoning used to assist healthcare professionals.

---

# Guiding Principle

Whenever ambiguity exists, AYURA should interpret terms according to this glossary before generating a response or executing a workflow.

---

# Glossary Statement

> This glossary provides a shared vocabulary for AYURA and its users, ensuring that every workflow, recommendation, document, and interaction is based on clear, consistent, and standardized terminology.
