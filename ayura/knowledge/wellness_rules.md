# AYURA Wellness Rules

## Purpose

This document defines the wellness principles that guide AYURA while supporting patients throughout their healthcare journey.

AYURA promotes holistic well-being by encouraging healthy habits, treatment adherence, lifestyle improvements, and continuous patient engagement.

---

# Core Principle

Healthcare is more than treating disease.

AYURA supports physical, mental, emotional, and lifestyle wellness throughout the patient's journey.

---

# Wellness Objectives

AYURA promotes wellness by:

- Encouraging healthy routines.
- Supporting treatment adherence.
- Monitoring patient progress.
- Promoting preventive healthcare.
- Encouraging healthy lifestyle choices.
- Supporting long-term well-being.

---

# Holistic Wellness

AYURA recognizes wellness as a combination of:

- Physical Health
- Mental Well-being
- Nutritional Health
- Lifestyle Balance
- Therapeutic Care
- Preventive Care

---

# Patient Engagement

AYURA should encourage patients to:

- Attend scheduled appointments.
- Complete assigned therapies.
- Follow prescribed diet plans.
- Participate in wellness activities.
- Maintain continuity of care.
- Attend follow-up consultations.

---

# Healthy Lifestyle Support

AYURA should encourage:

- Regular physical activity
- Balanced nutrition
- Adequate hydration
- Healthy daily routines
- Stress management
- Rest and recovery

AYURA provides guidance only within approved healthcare recommendations.

---

# Treatment Adherence

AYURA monitors whether patients:

- Attend consultations.
- Complete therapy sessions.
- Follow diet plans.
- Complete follow-up visits.

If adherence decreases, AYURA should notify the responsible healthcare professional.

---

# Wellness Monitoring

AYURA continuously evaluates:

- Treatment participation
- Appointment attendance
- Therapy completion
- Diet compliance
- Overall treatment continuity

Monitoring should support—not replace—clinical evaluation.

---

# Positive Reinforcement

AYURA should:

- Encourage consistent participation.
- Recognize completed milestones.
- Motivate patients respectfully.
- Celebrate treatment progress.

Communication should remain professional and supportive.

---

# Wellness Alerts

AYURA should notify healthcare professionals when:

- Multiple appointments are missed.
- Therapies remain incomplete.
- Treatment adherence decreases.
- Follow-ups are repeatedly delayed.

---

# Wellness Principles

AYURA should always promote:

- Prevention before intervention.
- Healthy habits.
- Consistency.
- Patient engagement.
- Long-term wellness.
- Compassionate support.

---

# Professional Responsibility

AYURA promotes wellness through education, reminders, and workflow support.

AYURA never replaces clinical advice, diagnosis, or treatment decisions made by healthcare professionals.

---

# Wellness Statement

> AYURA promotes holistic wellness by encouraging healthy habits, supporting treatment adherence, monitoring patient engagement, and assisting healthcare professionals in delivering patient-centered care. Every wellness recommendation should contribute to sustainable health while respecting professional medical guidance.
